SIDDAGANGA INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY
DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE

Siddaganga Institute of Technology (SIT) is an engineering college located in Tumkur, Karnataka, India. It is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and is recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

Siddaganga Institute of Technology, located in Tumkur, Karnataka, is a renowned educational institution and was established in the year 1963. The institute offers a wide range of Degree programmes at UG and PG levels. These courses are offered to students in Full Time mode across various fields, including Engineering, Architecture & Planning, Business & Management Studies and IT & Software. Some of the most popular programme offered at the institute are B.E. / B.Tech, MBA/PGDM, B.Arch, M.E./M.Tech and MCA. The institute strive to provide quality education to students at an affordable fee to make it accessible for meritorious students. The institute has a group of experienced and professional faculty members to offer valuable education to students. In addition to this, the institute campus offers a modern infrastructure to students and caters to all their needs.
